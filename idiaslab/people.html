Intelligent Big Data Analytics, Applications, and Systems

TThe UAlbany IDIAS Lab focuses on Socially Important Data Science. We develop models, algorithms, and systems to solve problems involving big, often networked data. We work closely with domain experts to address real--world Societal Challenges as well as solve challenging problems involving Online Social Networks.
